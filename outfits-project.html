How We Wear it - Lululemon

This project consisted of providing lululemon's customers with a way to find out how they could mix and match different products. The idea was to increase sales by giving customer's ideas on how to wear their lululemon goodies.

Some of the features of this project are infinite scrolling, social media integration and website tracking.

Technologies used on this project: Wordpress, HTML, Jquery, CSS, MySql, Omniture and Google Analytics.
